I wandered the world and found what I love to do.

UX blends what I enjoy most: problem solving and design.

I am perpetually learning. I’ve learned empathy from living around the world: Japan, Bolivia, Peru, and Vietnam. I’ve learned how to be analytical from my background as a biochemist. I’ve studied human nature through teaching in the Lower East Side of New York City. I’ve worked at a digital education start-up for the past few years where I was able to wear many hats, including product designer, project manager, and product manager.

I am addicted to crosswords and PokemonGo.

I use my past experiences to guide me in my design process: empathize, define, ideate, prototype and test.

I am looking to solve challenging problems in an environment where collaboration is valued.
