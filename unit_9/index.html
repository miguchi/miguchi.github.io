Succulents are us.

In 2010, three friends began a mission. Known collectively as the “plant ladies,” they searched for plants that they could love. As they went from country to country, they met plant-lovers as passionate as them. All of their succulents are grown on small organic farms owned by local farmers in countries such as Bolivia, Guam, and Vietnam with all proceeds going directly to the farmers.
